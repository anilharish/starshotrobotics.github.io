Transforming how machines see the world

We engineer a full range of perception and computer vision products essential for robotics and autonomy.

Our mission is to engineer sensors and machines to perceive the world better. When they can effectively sense, detect, analyze and respond to their environment, a whole new world of possibilities open. These solutions need strong understanding of their environment to act and evolve in real time.

We make this easy by allowing you to develop, test and deploy powerful and intelligent machines to the field. Our technology, sensors and software allow your robots or autonomous system to sense depth, navigate off-road terrains, segment natural scenes, objects, people and their actions.

DISCOVER OUR SOLUTIONS

Starshot robotics offers powerful solutions not only to solve your sensor, perception and autonomy needs but also trailblazing software and products to automate dull, dirty and dangerous tasks. Find what fits your needs.

Visual Perception

From 3D reconstruction, image segmentation, object recognition, volumetric tracking to visual odometry, 3D mapping, spacial detection, body tracking to name a few.

Autonomy

Multi-sensor integration, path planning localization and control. Including training neural networks to assist in data driven models to process sensor information.

Integrations

Allow us to help you build your solutions by integrating our software, API and SDKs with ROS, Unity, OpenCV, deep learning frameworks and many more tools and plugins.

Fleet Management

Management of robot fleet through dedicated cloud applications. We can help you keep tab on your core competencies by providing real time information over the air for indoor or outdoor machines.

Sensors and Components

Cannot find sensor on the market that meet your needs ? We undertake full development, deployment, service and life cycle management of custom components tailor made for your application.

Service and Support

We don't just making in inspiring, revolutionary and reliable products but firmly believe in providing execeptional customer service for all our services and products.
