Be the Future of Robotics

Robotics is an constantly evolving field with endless possibilities. This future-proof field means new opportunities for a surprising variety of skillsets. Join our team of multi-disciplinary, world class professionals who are building a commercial company from the core of robotics research and development.

If you want to take your ideas to the world you are in the right place. Help us transform the landscape of robotics in India and the world.

Life at Starshot Robotics

We are a diverse group of adventurers, industry veterans, creators, mentors and students. Our people bring unique talents, intelligence, and perspectives to the table. Every idea, improvement and invention from our team gets unbiased and comprehensive attention. It quickly brought to fruition, tested extensively and send it out to the world. You'll be surprised to see how fast we move from idea to prototype !

Curiosity helps us thrive in challenges associated with innovation. We don't fear failure but look at it as an opportunity to learn and grow. We strive for simple solutions and then phase in complexity where its demanding. We trust in each other and in each others work which is pivotal to solving hard problems.

Culture, Diversity and Inclusion

At Starshot Robotics we celebrate diversity. Our hiring process is focused on inclusiveness. Weather you hold in engineering degree or just concluded at high school or just hands on experience, we have a place for you.

We believe collaboration is the key to success. We cultivate teams that work effectively with one another throughout the organization. Robotics is a fundamentally interdisciplinary so inclusiveness across the organization at every stage of product development and decision making process is a key element of the company.
